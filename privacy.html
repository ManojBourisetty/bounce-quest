Privacy Policy

Bounce Quest • Last updated: 2026-06-12

Bounce Quest is an offline-first game. It is designed so that it never needs to send any information about you or your device anywhere.

Information we collect

None. Bounce Quest does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. The game contains no analytics, advertising, or tracking technologies of any kind.

Data stored on your device

Bounce Quest saves your level progress, star ratings, and best times locally on your device (using your browser's or app's local storage). This information stays on your device — it is never uploaded, and we have no access to it. You can erase it at any time by clearing the app's data/storage or reinstalling the app.

Permissions

Bounce Quest does not request access to your camera, microphone, location, contacts, photos, or any other device data or permissions.

Children's privacy

Bounce Quest is designed to be fun for players of all ages, including children. Because the game collects no personal information from any user, including children under 13, it complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations in other regions.

Third parties

Bounce Quest does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics, or advertising networks.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
